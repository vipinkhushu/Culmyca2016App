  • MR AND MISS CULMYCA

    Unlike its contemporaries, here it is not just about your style quotient or vogue. As they say, "Beauty with brain is an absolutely deadly combination", we believe: "A chivalrous knight is equally lethal!" So join in and prove your mettle to get hold of the most coveted title of the fiesta! The battle for the title will go through a series of 3 competitive rounds.
    RULES :
    ROUND I
    Register for the contest at: http://ocul.in/faceofculmyca
    Fill out the details and other necessary information required.
    Submit your photograph that best describes you, with 'culmyca' appearing anywhere in background. The most creative photographs will be scored higher. The photographs will be uploaded on our Facebook page where you need to make sure you get as many likes as possible. Likes on facebook page shall account for 50% of the total score.
    Instructions:
    1. Applicant must be a student of a recognised institution.
    2. Age limit: 18-25 years.
    3. Deadline- 11:59PM of 1st March.

    ROUND II
    Selected candidates from ROUND I will mail a video that showcases their talent in the best way possible. All talents are appreciated. Mail us a glimpse of it at: faceofculmyca@gmail.com.
    Instructions:
    1. Deadline-11:59PM of 9th March
    2. Video duration should not exceed 60 seconds.

    ROUND III
    Candidates qualifying ROUNDS I and II will go through a final showdown on-stage where they will face our judges. They will be judged on the basis of a ramp walk and their answers to various tricky hypothetical situations. Buckle up for an exciting face-off.
    Instructions:
    Reach the venue on time.
