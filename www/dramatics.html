11th – 13th March 2016
DRAMATICS
  • LYRICAL PLAY

    A musical melodrama of never ending love stories. It is a treat to admirers of shero shayari. This act will take you to the ages of the maharaja, along with "bhule bisre geet".
    VENUE:
    11th March,2016(Day 1)
    Main Stage
    Timings:5.30 pm-6.00 pm

  • MIME

    The historical theatrical play of suggesting action, character, or emotion without words, using only gesture, expression, and movement. But in the act without voice, creating a wonderful story for the audience to enjoy and cherish
    VENUE:
    12th March,2016(Day 2)
    Main Stage
    Timings:6.30 pm

    Register

  • STAGE PLAY

    For the ones who enjoy proper theatre, with unlimited props to use, and whole stage to utilize the participants completely immersed into the character. This event is a treat to watch for the ones who love the drama.
    Team Size: 8 to 24 (including musicians)
    Time Limit: 15-25 minutes(empty stage to empty stage)
    Judging criteria: Acting, Voice (sync, modulation, diction), Screenplay, Script, Crowd interaction and overall impact(rhythm, music, presentation)
    Music instruments : atmost 3 membrane instruments, Harmonium, Manjeera.
    Rules and Regulations:
    • The team size represents the number of people registered as a team. Only these shall be allowed to perform the street play.
    • Minimum of 8 actors are to be included in the team.
    • No electrical appliances shall be allowed during the performance, whether inside or outside the circle of performance.
    • Only live music is allowed. Teams will have to bring their own instruments - no instrument shall be provided. • Language of the performance should be Hindi/English or both. However short sub passages in other languages are allowed.
    • Any kind of fluid, live animals, flame, heavy object or any material which has a possibility of damaging the stage is not allowed.
    • Teams will be penalised if they exceed the time limit.
    • Plays can be based on any theme but since performance is being held at public venues, any content directed against a certain political group or religious community is strictly prohibited.
    • Obscenity (at the discretion of judges) of any kind is not allowed and may lead to disqualification.
    • Any kind of plagiarism and profanity will lead to immediate disqualification.
    • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
    VENUE:
    12th March,2016(Day 2)
    Main Stage
    Timings:1.30 pm-4.30 pm

    Register
