  • HOVER RUSH

    Culmyca Brings to you the most realistic and thrilling Hover racing experience. No braking or reverse - just a throttle, steering and your skill. So outdrive and outrace your rivals to win exciting prizes!
    RULES:
    1. A team may consist of a maximum of 4 members. Students from different colleges can form a team.
    2. Event structure:
    • Round 1: Qualifying Round
    Each team will have to race their bots on the provided track for one lap and the lap time will be taken into consideration. Top 3 teams, on the basis of minimum lap time will qualify for the second round.
    • Round 2: Hurdles round
    Top teams have to race with hurdles on the tracks simultaneously and the one who completes first will be the winner.
    3. Model specifications:
    Maximum dimension of hovercraft should not exceed 50cm X 50cm X 50cm. There is no limitation of weight. Model should be handmade. Hovercraft assembled from kits are strictly not allowed. Metal propellers are not allowed.
    4. Only one attempt in arena will be entertained. No other chance will be provided.
    5. Rules and regulations can be changed/updated by organizers without any prior notice. Do check the site regularly.
    6. The decision of organizers is final and cannot be challenged by participants.
    7. For the sake of participants, only 2.4GHz 6 channel remote and lithium-ion batteries may be provided and it should be returned to coordinators after the event. Any damage to them will be charged by participants.
    8. Arena design will not be shown before the event.
    VENUE:
    11th March,2016(Day 1)
    Shakuntlam
    Timings:10.00 am-1.00 pm

  • PUZZLFIE

    An interesting event with a number of captivating rounds. Here you'll be taking selfies but with a technical touch! Don't forget there's enough suspense to boggle your brain!
    INSTRUCTIONS:
    The participants will be asked to make a team of maximum 3 members. If they are unable to form a team, the teams will be made randomly by the judges/coordinators. In each round, points will be given for performing the tasks in least time. This event PUZZLFIE comprises of 3 rounds in which teams will compete.

    FIRST ROUND –
    In this round a member of every team has to pick a slip containing a name of an area inside the campus. Teams should try to include Maximum number of people in their GROUPIE clicked at the given place in the campus. Only the people facing towards the camera will be considered in the selfie. They have to complete this task in a given time.
    No. of Points = (No. of people in the GROUPIE - Extra time they take (in min))
    SECOND ROUND -
    In this round, a team has to solve a puzzle. The puzzle won’t be disclosed. Puzzle can be of any type. The round may be followed by an elimination. No. of Points = 30 – Time taken to solve the puzzle. If hint is given then -1 is awarded.
    THIRD ROUND –
    In this round the teams will have to solve a quiz (eg. – a crossword). The quiz will be provided to the team and not to every single person in the team.
    The number of questions they have to solve = Given no. of questions – [(No.of people in the GROUPIE)/3 ]
    No. of Points = (Percentage marks)/10 + ( 30 – Time taken to solve the quizzes)
    For eg. Marks obtained are 50/100, then
    %marks = 50,
    Points = (50/10) + ( 30 – Time taken to solve the quizzes)
    TERMS AND RULES:
    1. Each team should have a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 3 participants.
    2. Prize money can be changed.
    3. Prize is to be shared by the winning team.
    4. In the First Round, the people must face the camera. Points will not be awarded for the person not facing the camera.
    5. In the First Round, at least one team member should be there in the photo. Also the team members will not be counted in the photo.
    6. Maximum time for each round is 20 minutes which may be changed according to the situation.
    7. Every round is played among the teams and not among individuals.
    8. There is no elimination in the first round.
    9. Elemination in the other rounds depends on judges.
    10.Decision of the judges will be final.
    11.We reserve the right to change any rule without any prior notice.
    VENUE:
    11th March,2016(Day 1)
    Indoor 1
    Timings:10.30 am-12.30 pm

  • CONTRAPTION

    Have you ever made a mechanism that appears strange or unnecessarily complicated ? If yes, then discover design and demonstrate your talent. For Contraption all you need is planning, innovation and a perfect use of material!
    DESCRIPTION:
    Contraption is the Chain Reaction undergoing a number of steps, showing different mechanisms and using elementary engineering principles to perform tasks.
    Problem Statement:
    Use all of the given resources creatively to accomplish particular goals.

    RULES AND REGULATIONS:
    General Rules -
    • Each team can have a maximum of 4 members.
    • Students must carry proof of identity issued by their institution.
    • No participant shall be allowed to be a part of more than one team.

    Contraption Rules -
    1. An initial setup time of 40 mins will be given to each team.
    2. No hand touch is allowed in the middle of the contraption.
    3. Time of 10 mins will be given to display the contraption.
    4. Points will be given to every energy conversion and for each step in the contraption.
    5. Points will be given for maximum utilisation of the given materials.
    6. Complexity of contraption will also be the criteria for judging the same.
    7. Points will be given for each completed task or objective.

    Judging Criteria:
    1. No. of steps in contraption.
    2. No. of energy conversions.
    3. Types of energy conversions.
    4. Complexity of overall contraption.
    5. Materials used.
    6. Number of objectives achieved.

    The decision made by the event coordinators will be final. Any argument over it will not be entertained.
    VENUE:
    12th March,2016(Day 2)
    Indoor 2
    Timings:11.30 am-2.30 pm

    Soar high through the sky of YMCA. Let free your gliders and the parachute water rockets!

    RULES for gliders:
