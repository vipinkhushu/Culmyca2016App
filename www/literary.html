  • JUST A MINUTE

    It is a one minute challenge to test your adaptability, spontaneity, language and fluency. If you have the skills, then this is the place to flaunt.
    RULES :
    1. Language: English.
    2. INDIVIDUAL participation.
    3. In the group of participants, one starts speaking on the topic given on the spot.
    4. A mistake of any kind to upset the rules of British English leads to other participants banging the table.
    5. The one who bangs first gets to point the flaw and continue from there on.
    6. The JAM master is God.
    7. In case of any doubts, refer to the rules mentioned above.
    VENUE:
    11th March,2016(Day 1)
    Conference Hall
    Timings:10.00 am-1.00 pm

    Register

  • WITSWILLA

    Witsvilla is a three level competition that will challenge you at each level. Success awaits you at the other end of a journey, which comprises of a Group Discussion, Extempore and Turncoat.
    RULES :
    1. It is a bilingual individual event.
    2. The first round is a "Group discussion" where 6 to 8 people on a table will be a given a topic to discuss about.
    3. The next round is an "Extempore" round. Each participant will be a given a topic on the spot to speak on.
    4. Each speaker will be given 2 minute thinking time and 2 minute speaking time.
    5. The last round will be of "Turncoat". Each speaker will have to speak in favour and against a particular topic.
    6. Each speaker will get 2 minute thinking time and 2 minute speaking time (1 minute in favour and 1 minute against).
    VENUE:
    12th March,2016(Day 2)
    Conference Hall
    Timings:10.00 am-1.00 pm

    Register

  • OPEN MIC POETRY

    Do you aspire to mesmerize the world with your poetic lyrical enigma? Do you believe in the magic your words can unveil? Here is a platform for all the budding poets to live their aspirations.
    It is a bilingual event (entries can either be in Hindi or English).
    RULES:
    1. The 1st Round is a preliminary offline round. All the participants should send their poem before 1st March 2016 at ymcaust.ananya@gmail.com along with thier name, contact number and email id. (The poem should be sent either in pdf or word file.)
    2. Top finalists will be selected and they will recite thier poem on the stage.
    3. The expression and manner of recitation will also be marked.
    4. The decision of the judges will be final.
    VENUE:
    11th March,2016(Day 1)
    MMC
    Timings:1.30 am-4.30 pm

    Register

  • INSURRECTION

    A debater has conviction in his speech, logic in his arguments, accuracy in facts and confidence in his delivery! Do you claim a mastery at it? Insurrection awaits you!
    It is a bilingual event. There will be two rounds.
    RULES :
    1. Each team should have two members.
    2. The 1st round will be a conventional debate round.
    3. The topic will be released 24 hours prior to the event.
    4. Speaking time for each speaker will be 3+1 minutes.
    5. Each team will be subjected to two interjections (one by the judges and one by the audience).
    6. Top three teams will qualify for the final round.
    7. The final round is an extempore round.
    8. Each speaker will get 2 minute thinking time and 2 minute speaking time.
    9. One interjection by the judges.
    10. The decision of the judges will be final.
    VENUE:
    13th March,2016(Day 3)
    Conference Hall
    Timings:10.00 am-1.30 pm
