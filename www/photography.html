    RULES:
    1. The photographs should be clicked inside the YMCA Campus during the Culmyca 2016.
    2. Individual entries will be accepted.
    3. An individual can send maximum three entries.

A watermarked photo may not be honored in the competition (at the judges sole discretion) if the photographer is unable or unavailable to provide a pristine, watermark-free version of the image when asked.

    10. Take care :P

    11th March,2016(Day 1)

  • PHOTOBUCKET

    3 days of immense craziness, 3 days of insanity and 3 days of tremendous fun. The streets dazzling in bright colors and shimmering lights, are full of some of the wackiest characters and wildest personalities. In short, the perfect setting for a Photograph and Video!!! Take snaps, shoot your favorite moments. Deliver them to us and win exciting prizes.
    Team Size: Solo
    Round(s): Direct Finals
    Theme: Necessities of Life, Trapped, Golden Moments of Zindagi, Carnival, Dreams, Digital India, Make in India. Themes are open to interpretation.
    You can send a pdf document containing only text to describe your photos or thematic interpretation, though there will be no points for this description though.
    Judging Criteria: Relevance to theme, Composition and arrangement, Focus and sharpness, Lighting, Creativity.
    Deadline for submission of entries is 13th March 2016.
    GENERAL RULES:
    1. Camera Resolution should be at least 3.2 mega pixels.
    2. Photographs must be clicked on a date later than 5 th March, 2016.
    3. One without EXIF data (and hence the date) will not be accepted. So, make sure that your camera date is set before you click a picture.
    4. In photo editing only cropping and adjustment of hue/saturation; brightness/contrast levels are allowed. Photo-morphing, manipulations or local editing is not allowed.
    5. Participants have to submit both the edited and original entries for judging.
    6. Do not include your credentials (name, address etc) in the picture or the frame. Such photos will not be considered for the contest.
    7. Once the best photographs are shortlisted, the concerned participants would be asked to submit a scanned copy of their identity cards.
    8. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
    9. Any sign of plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
    VENUE:
    11th,12th,13th March,2016(Day 1,2,3)
    Timings:All Time

  • CINE FIESTA

    “The most honest form of filmmaking, is to make a film for yourself.” - Peter Jackson. Do you ever find yourself immersed in a world where you enter others’ minds and see the world as another person sees it? Do you want your vision to create magic on the 70mm screen? Start thinking. Get a Story. Grab your cast, camera and director’s hat. Create something that will never erase. Store a piece of your thought in the grandest of all means humans have ever created- A Movie!
    Team size: At most 15 (including the actors)
    Round(s): Online eliminations
    Eliminations: Online entry to be submitted.
    Time Limit: 5 to 15 minutes
    Theme:There is no theme for this round.
    Deadline: Entries should be last submitted by 13th March 2016 to jhalakcaptus@gmail.com with subject 'CINEFIESTA'
    Judging Criteria: Editing, Cinematography, Script, Acting, Sound design and Overall impact.
    GENERAL RULES:
    1. The movies should be either in Hindi or English or both. If you choose to make a film in Hindi, it must contain English subtitles. No other languages will be allowed.
    2. Please note that we will not be responsible for postal delays, losses or damages–make sure you send in entries sufficiently before the deadline.
    3. You may use any video camera you deem suitable for shooting the movie. However, we strongly discourage low-resolution cameras, including webcams and mobile-phone cameras.
    4. The content of the movie should be appropriate for public screening and thus should have no vulgarity.
    5. Obscenity (at the discretion of the judges) of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
    6. Entries must be the original work of the entrants and must not infringe third-party's rights.
    7. Please avoid photo slideshows, remixes, spoofs and advertisements.
    8. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited. Sound track can be borrowed, but with due credit.
    VENUE:
    11th March,2016(Day 1)
    MBA Hall
    Timings:11.00 am-1.00 pm
